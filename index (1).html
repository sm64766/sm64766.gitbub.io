The Bearded Dragon

The name bearded dragon coined for the famous beard on the underside of the throat has become one of the most popular exotic pets in the United States since the 1990s. Their popularity has not come as a surprise, due to the fact that they are one of the most easy reptiles to care for. This native Australia lizard is well known for its easy maintenance, its happy-go-lucky attitude, and its longevity! As cool as they look, here are some fascinating facts about one of the cutest reptiles ever:

Fun Facts

  • The "beard" on the bearded dragon turns black when the reptile is stressed or sees a potential rival.
  • Commonly referred to as "beardies", they wave their arms at each other to show species recognition or to demonstrate submission.
  • Bearded Dragons can live to around 10 years.
  • Bearded dragons cannot grow back their tails like most lizards.
  • .Although they are mainly sedentary, these lizards can run up to 9 miles per hour!
